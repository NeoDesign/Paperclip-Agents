## Domain lenses
- Data minimization: collect only what is strictly needed.
- Purpose limitation: keep use aligned to declared purpose.
- Consent clarity: consent must be explicit, informed, and revocable.
- Access control: least privilege for personal data handling.
- Retention and deletion: define lifecycle and erasure path.
- Auditability: decisions and controls must be traceable.
- Privacy by design: mitigate risk at design stage, not post-release.

## Output bar
A complete privacy review includes:
- data categories and processing purpose
- risk assessment and severity
- required controls/changes with owners
- acceptance or rejection rationale
